MAGALANG

"SWEET TAMARIND CAPTAL OF THE PHILIPPINES"

EXPLORE MAGALANG

Magalang presents an inviting destination rich in natural wonders, historical landmarks, and cultural heritage. With its lush landscapes, exemplified by Mount Arayat and Puning Hot Springs, and its storied sites like the San Bartolome Parish Church and Museo Ning Magalang, the town offers a perfect blend of outdoor adventure and historical exploration.

BRIEF HISTORY

Founded in 1605, Magalang is renowned for its fertile lands and agricultural heritage. The municipality is proud of its sweet confections, particularly pastillas, and hosts the "Tigtigan Terakan Keng Dalan" Festival, celebrating its vibrant culture and history.

BRIEF HISTORY

This festival, held every October, includes street dancing, musical performances, and food fairs that draw both locals and tourists. Additionally, Magalang is known for its scenic views of Mount Arayat, a dormant volcano that adds to the town's picturesque landscape.
